Welcome to the World of Kiwi!

Kiwi is a small, flightless bird that is native to New Zealand. They have a fuzzy brown exterior with a vibrant green interior and are known for their unique appearance and quirky personality.

Despite their small size, Kiwis play a big role in the ecosystem, as they are important seed dispersers for many of New Zealand's native plants.

Unfortunately, Kiwis are also threatened by habitat loss and introduced predators, so conservation efforts are underway to help protect these beloved birds.

So come and learn more about these amazing creatures, and discover why Kiwis are a national symbol of New Zealand!
